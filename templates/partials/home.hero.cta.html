What is Nix?

Nix is a powerful package manager that makes package management reliable and reproducible. It provides atomic upgrades and rollbacks, side-by-side installation of multiple versions of a package, multi-user package management and easy setup of build environments.

Learn more about NixOS

What is a NUG?

A Nix User Group (NUG) is a group of people who are interested in Nix and related technologies. NUGs are a great way to meet other Nix users, share knowledge and experiences, and learn about new developments in the Nix ecosystem.
